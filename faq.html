Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to the reception?

The dress code for our wedding reception is semi-formal/cocktail attire. We recommend ladies to wear cocktail or full-length dresses, and gentlemen to wear a suit and tie or a sports coat. Please do not wear red, white, or ivory attire.

What will the weather be like in San Francisco in October?

The weather is typically high 50s, low 60s in the evening. The venue is located by the water so the evening could be a bit chilly.

Will the reception be indoors or outdoors?

Cocktail hour will take place outdoors on the patio and the following events of the reception will take place indoors. Access to the patio will be available for the entire evening and will have outdoor heaters.

Are children welcome?

Our reception is going to be an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us.
