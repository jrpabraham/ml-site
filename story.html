LEA and MARK

Our Story

This love story began in Japanese class. The year was 2011. The professor had students in the class turn to the person sitting next to them and take turns practicing ordering at a fast food restaurant. Mark turned to the girl next to him and asked for a cheeseburger with onions, pickles, ketchup, mustard, lettuce, bun, patty, and no cheese. That's how Mark met Lea. As a couple, Mark and Lea have been together for eight years and going on nine.

Mark proposed to Lea on May 30, 2022 as the twist ending to an escape room mystery. Lea's brother, David, captured the thrilling moment on video. They're excited to marry and begin their next adventure!
